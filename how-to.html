How to cook Sweet Potato

When is a potato not a potato? When it's a sweet potato. Altough a sweet potatoes is stary root vegetable it is not a part of the potato family. Sweet potatoe come in diffrent veriaties, from a bright orange flesh to a pink, yellow or even purple flesh. When selecting sweet potatoes, choose ones of a similar colour and size. They should be firm, with smooth, unblemished skin and no holes or nicks. Sweet potatoes can be boiled, roasted or fried. When roasted, they should be place in a medium-hot oven for 45 minutes.

How cook the perfect Pasta

Smooth on the outside and with a bite. Cooking the perfect pasta, al dent, is not a hard as you think. All you need to di is foloow a few simple rules and you will have perfectly cooked pasta everytime. It is important to cook pasta in a large pan, you want to give pasta the room to move and expand while cooking. The cooking water should be as salty as the sea, this will give your pasta some flavor as it cooks. To check that the pasta is cookes, take a piece out the water and taste it - it should be soft with a slightly al dente centre.

How to make a wobbly Panna Cotta

Panna Cotta is a tradition Italian dessert, it is a simple mixture of sweetend cream and gelatine. A good panna cotta is on that is only just set, and can stand on it own on a plate. Panna cotta is usualy served in a glass and/or plate as well as a plated dessert. A panna cotta that wiggles on the sponn when eaten is a sign of a panna cotta with the perfect consistency.
