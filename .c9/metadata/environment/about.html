Chris Harms

\n

\n Around the age of 15 I when first dipped my toes in the culinary world. I world as a bar tender at a hotel, I used to make and serve drinks. \n At that time I watched a lot of cooking show on Food-Network, my favorite show at the time was from Emeril Lagasse. \n The way that he cooks was with a feeling and not by following a recipe. I liked this very much, because you cook by using your taste-buds and not by reading. \n When I started school, it was a sensory overload. I got to learn so much about produce and cooking. \n At that time I didnâ€™t have a clue about how it would be to work in a kitchen or everything that is possible to do with food.\n

\n

\n A few years later, and some other working experience I ended up a Chefz in Oosterhout where I currently work. \n At Chefz I currently work as Head-Chef, they had really low expectation because all other previous chefs were underperforming. \n After 2 months of working there the owner and the customer liked the food that I was making. Then was where I official got the keys from the kitchen. \n I am now able to run the kitchen as I want to.\n
