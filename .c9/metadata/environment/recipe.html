Tuna Tataki

\n

\n This salad is made by briefly marinating a piece of tuna in red wine vinger and than searing it off in a hot pan. The tuna is than slides thinly and placed on a bed of rucola, orange segments and pickled onion. The following ingredientsare needed:
\n\n

    \n
  • 1 red onion
    • \n
  • 1 cup red wine vinegar
    • \n
  • 1 cup water
    • \n
  • ⅔ cup sugar
    • \n
  • ½ cup soja sauce
    • \n
  • ¾ cup sweet chilli sauce
    • \n
  • ¼ cup sesame oil

  • ¼ cup chopped cilantro

How to Make Tiramisu

\n

\n Tiramisu is the perfect romantic occasion dessert. It is rich and deeply satisfyling, yet remarkably light in texture. A titamisu is basically layers of mascarpone mixture with layers of espresso-dipped ladyfingers. This recipe can easly be multiplied\n for larger portions. For the tiramisu you will need the following:
\n\n

    \n
  • ½ cup espresso
    • \n
  • 2 tbsp Marsala wine
    • \n
  • 12 ladyfingers
    • \n
  • 2 large egg yolks
    • \n
  • 2 tbsp plus 1 tsp sugar
    • \n
  • ½ tsp vanilla extract
    • \n
  • ¾ cup (6oz) mascarpone cheese
    • \n
  • 2 large egg whites
    • \n
  • Cocoa for dusting
    • \n

Chocolate Lava Cake

\n

\n Lava cake one of the most impressive cakes, know for it's lava-like center, delicious molten chocolate. The difficulty with this dessert is the timming; too long in the oven and you end up with no lava, \n and not long enough in the oven you end up with a souppy mess. Therefore, timming is the key!. For this cake you'll need:
\n

    \n
  • 3 large eggs
    • \n
  • 4 tbsp sugar
    • \n
  • 170 gr dark chocolate
