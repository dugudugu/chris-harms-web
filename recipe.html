Tuna Tataki

This salad is made by briefly marinating a piece of tuna in red wine vinger and than searing it off in a hot pan. The tuna is than slides thinly and placed on a bed of rucola, orange segments and pickled onion. The ingredients are:

  • 1 red onion
  • 1 cup red wine vinegar
  • 1 cup water
  • ⅔ cup sugar
  • ½ cup soja sauce
  • ¾ cup sweet chilli sauce
  • ¼ cup sesame oil
  • Juice of 2 limes
  • ¼ cup chopped cilantro
  • Black & white sesame seeds
  • Rucola
  • Orange segments

Quick Sweet Potato and Coconut Milk Soup

Sweet potato is one of those recipes that everyone should have in their back-pocket. It's really simple, delicious and healthy. This soup can be made in just 15 minutes. If you can peel and dice vegetables, you can make this. The ingredients are:

  • 900g sweet potato
  • 270g carrots
  • 1 cup of coconut milk
  • 1 large onion
  • 1 whole chili
  • ½ tsp powdered ginger
  • ½ tsp powdered cumin
  • 6 cups of water
  • A few springs of fresh coriander
  • 3 tbsp olive oil

Rigatoni Al Segreto

Rigatoni Al Segreto used to be a closely guarded recipe of the than most famous Italian Restaurant in New York City. After the restaurant closed, the recipe was leaked, but not all the quantaties were correct. But here is a recipe for one of the version of this pasta dish. The ingredients are:

  • 4 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 cup diced onion
  • 2 or 3 cloves of garlic
  • A pich or red pepper flakes (optional)
  • 1 can (28oz) tomatoes
  • ½ cup water
  • Small handful of fresh basil leaves
  • 1 cup grated Parmigiano-Reggiano
  • 4 tbsp butter
  • Rigatoni pasta

How to Make Tiramisu

Tiramisu is the perfect romantic occasion dessert. It is rich and deeply satisfyling, yet remarkably light in texture. A titamisu iis basically layers of mascarpone mixture with layers of espresso-dipped ladyfingers. This recipe can easly be multiplied for larger portions. The ingredients are:

  • ½ cup espresso
  • 2 tbsp Marsala wine
  • 12 ladyfingers
  • 2 large egg yolks
  • 2 tbsp plus 1 tsp sugar
  • ½ tsp vanilla extract
  • ¾ cup (6oz) mascarpone cheese
  • 2 large egg whites
  • Cocoa for dusting
  • Dark chocolate for shaving
