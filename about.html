Chris Harms

Around the age of 15 is when I first dipped my toes in the culinary world. At that time I watched a lot of cooking show on Food-Network, my favorite show at the time was from Emeril Lagasse. The way that he cooks was with a feeling and not by following a recipe. I liked this very much because you cook by using your taste-buds and not by reading.

When I started school, it was a sensory overload. I got to learn so much about produce and cooking. At that time I didnâ€™t have a clue about how it would be to work in a kitchen or everything that is possible to do with food.

A few years later, and more working experience I ended up at Chefz in Oosterhout. At Chefz I currently work as Head-Chef. When I first started, they had a really low expectation because all other previous chefs were underperforming. After 2 months of working there, the owner and the customers liked the food that I was making. At that moment is where I officially got the keys to the kitchen. I am now able to run the kitchen as I want to.

About

Chris Harms is currently Head Chef at Chefz in Oosterhout, The Netherland. He has been cooking for over 10 years and would like to share some recipes with you.
